The Venue

This year's event will be hosted in partnership with the lovely people at Wisdom Gaming, who have graciously chosen to sponsor the costs to reserve their venue and several of their rental PCs. Wisdom has a reputable brand as one of the major esports organizations in the region, with plenty of experience in hosting events similar to our own.

About Wisdom (from wisdom.gg)

Wisdom Gaming Studios is the premiere destination for esports and gaming in the Midwest. Our 18,000 sq. foot state-of-the-art broadcast studio and esports venue is located on Level 4 East at Mall of America. It features seating for a live studio audience, gaming and streamer booths, a merch store called Side Quest and The High Ground which serves food and drinks.

Setups

For this event, Wisdom will be providing 22 rental PCs and montors built for high-performance gaming. These will be reserved on a first come first serve basis, so be sure to register early if you plan on grabbing one. Otherwise you may bring your own computer, though we will not be assuming responsibility for any risks or damages involved in this process. Furthermore, all attendees will be responsible for bringing their own peripherials, as Wisdom will not be providing any. This includes:

For software, Wisdom will also cover almost everything. Copies of osu! and OpenTabletDriver will be installed on every PC prior to the event, and you will be free to download any beatmaps or skins you desire while there. However, any additional software you wish to install on the provided PCs will have to be evaluated on a case by case basis.

Food

Since the venue is located within the Mall of America, there will be plenty of resturants just minutes away for pretty much any craving that strikes your fancy. Additionally, Wisdom hosts its own resturant and bar on the top floor of the venue, known as The High Ground. This is a little more intimate than the mall food courts, sporting comfortable seating and a nice view of the mall from above.
