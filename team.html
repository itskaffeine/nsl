The Team

This event is 100% community driven, organized by no more than a handful of dedicated individuals and a love of the game. Our team doesn't earn a profit and never intends to, but we're always looking for more hands on deck. If you're up for a challenge and would like to help out with organization, consider reaching out through Discord to learn how you can get involved with the event. We look forward to hearing from you!
